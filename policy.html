1. Overview

Welcome to Tank Adventure! This document outlines our privacy policy and terms of service for the Tank Adventure game. Tank Adventure is a 2D tank battle game with wave-based combat and skill progression, designed for mobile and desktop web browsers.

2. Privacy Policy

2.1 Information We Collect

Tank Adventure is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide the best gaming experience:

  • Local Storage Data: Game progress, settings, and statistics are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or process any personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details
  • No Account Registration: The game does not require account creation or login
  • No Server Data: Game data is not transmitted to or stored on external servers

2.2 How We Use Information

The local storage data is used exclusively for:

  • Saving your game progress and achievements
  • Remembering your game settings and preferences
  • Tracking statistics for gameplay improvements
  • Providing a personalized gaming experience

2.3 Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. All game data remains on your device.

2.4 Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, data security is managed by your device's operating system and browser security features.

2.5 Data Retention

Game data is retained until you:

  • Clear your browser's storage/cache
  • Uninstall the game
  • Manually delete game data through browser settings
